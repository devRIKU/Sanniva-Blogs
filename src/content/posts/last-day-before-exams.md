Today marks the final day before the dreaded final exams begin. I see this as my last taste of freedom before I’m fully locked into my studies. Tomorrow starts with my A.I. exam, and with AI being squeezed into every corner of the internet, I bet everyone will get full marks. Even Zournel, the very website and app where I’m writing this, was made by AI and is packed with AI features.

I’ve already studied for nine hours today. Do you understand how long that actually is? At least I still have a little time to write down my thoughts. Tomorrow is actually a double-header because I have my Bengali exam as well. I don't hate the language, but I’ve found that if you force anything on me, I’ll eventually start to resent it. My relationship with Bengali hasn't always been the best, but I am trying to learn—and I'm getting better at it every single day.
