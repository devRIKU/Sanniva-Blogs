Artificial intelligence is no longer just a sci-fi concept. It is a tool that creatives are using every day to enhance their workflows and explore new possibilities.

Let us look at how AI is shaping the future of art and design.

## Collaborating with Machines

AI is not here to replace artists; it's here to augment them. By handling repetitive tasks, AI frees up human creators to focus on the big picture.

1.  **Ideation:** Generating hundreds of concepts in seconds.
2.  **Refinement:** Upscaling, color correcting, and polishing.
3.  **Exploration:** Combining styles that humans might never think to mix.
